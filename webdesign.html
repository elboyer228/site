Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my web design services and process

How long does it take to build a website?

Starter and Professional websites typically take up to 2 weeks, while Advanced websites may require up to 4 weeks. The timeline depends on the complexity of the features and the level of responsiveness required. Clear communication and timely feedback help keep the project on schedule. A detailed timeline will be provided after our initial meetings.

What do I need to provide before we start?

If your brand identity is already defined, you can send me all the digital assets you'd like to include ‚Äî such as your logo, fonts, color palette, images, product information, and any existing content. If not, we can develop your visual identity together to ensure it aligns with your business aesthetic and values.

What if I need to make changes after the website is finished?

It depends on the type of changes you need: for small updates (text, images, bug fixes), I offer maintenance support included in your package or as needed. For larger updates (new features, extra pages, major design changes), I offer flexible maintenance plans or hourly rates, depending on your requirements.

Can you help with domain name and hosting?

Absolutely! I assist with the setup of your domain and hosting as part of every package. However, domain and hosting fees are not included in my pricing. I recommend Infomaniak for its reliability and excellent performance.

How does payment work?

I typically work with a 50/50 payment split: 50% upfront to confirm the project and begin work, and 50% upon completion, once you're fully satisfied with the final result. If needed, I'm open to discussing alternative arrangements depending on the scope and timeline.

Do you work with clients outside of Switzerland?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide! While I'm based in Switzerland, I can collaborate effectively through video calls, email, and project management tools. Distance is never a barrier to creating great websites.
